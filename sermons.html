Sermons

    Hilarion: Sermon on Law and Grace (1050)
  • Binary structure: Old Testament is law, New Testament is grace, freedom and salvation
  • Parable about two wives of Abraham: Slave Hagar and her son Ishmael; free Sarah and her free son Isaac; Jews associated with Ishamel/Hagar and Christianity is about future, Sarah and Isaac
  • Hilarion praises newly baptized Rus as Christian kingdom of freedom. The political point of the sermon - Rus and its princes are the new Christian kingdom of the future (Vladimir and Yaroslav)
