Poor Liza (1792)

    Features of Sentimentalist tale
  • Concern with human emotions, in addition to reason
  • Concern with authenticity, hence first-person narration
  • Telling names (inherited from Neoclassicism) i.e. Erast
  • Common people become heroes, even women
  • Nature is a character and mirrors human emotions i.e. A storm happens when Liza loses her virginity (pathetic fallacy)
