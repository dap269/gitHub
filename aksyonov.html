Vasilyi Aksyonov

  • Youth prose writer: focus on language of young people, youth jargon, use of foreign words (especially English), invocation of popular culture and athletic icons (Wilt Chamberlain - Zvezdnyi bilet, novel published in Yunost literary journal)
  • Focus on protagonist's subjectivity
  • Sexuality, drinking
  • Denial of teleology - flight without any purpose; compare to Tale of a Real Man
  • Third space of freedom (space of flight) - neither center nor periphery
